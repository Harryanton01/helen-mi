Performed with Anastasia Mina as part of the Lychee performance event, 2017

Fanatic was one of a series of performances investigating ways in which meaning is dessimenated. Using translation as a device to interrupt understanding, the struggle to deliver meaning using words makes the essential element of communication more apparent. The attempt to create a connection with others becomes an expression of love, which belies the often antagonistic content of the words, and which endures despite the inherent failure of the attempt.
